The Aid Transparency Tracker

The Aid Transparency Tracker is an online platform built by Publish What You Fund to monitor progress made by organisations in making their aid information more transparent. The platform includes three separate tools:

  1. An Implementation Schedules analysis tool which assesses all IATI/Common Standard schedules published to date by organisations as part of their commitment to publish aid information to a common standard by the end of 2015;
  2. A Data Quality tool which assesses the quality of data being published in IATI XML format;
  3. A survey tool which assesses aid information published by organisations, in formats other than IATI XML, based on survey data collected manually for the annual Aid Transparency Index.
